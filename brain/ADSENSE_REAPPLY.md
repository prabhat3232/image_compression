# AdSense reapplication checklist (Phase 4)

Wait **2–4 weeks** after Phase 1 content is live and indexed before reapplying.

## Before you apply

1. Confirm **30+ indexed URLs** in Google Search Console (home, trust pages, 10 tool pages, guides, glossary).
2. Submit **sitemap**: `https://fileshrinkr.com/sitemap.xml`
3. Validate **Rich Results** for FAQ/HowTo on key landing pages.
4. Ensure **ads.txt** resolves: `https://fileshrinkr.com/ads.txt`
5. Privacy Policy mentions **AdSense**, Analytics, cookies, and uploads.
6. Contact form works (or mailto fallback is visible).

## Site quality signals

- Original long-form copy on each landing page (not duplicate boilerplate).
- Clear navigation: Home, Tools, Guides, Glossary, About, Contact.
- No thin “doorway” pages; each tool page has compressor + FAQs.

## Reapply

1. Search Console → confirm no manual actions.
2. AdSense → Sites → add or review `fileshrinkr.com`.
3. If rejected again, note reason and expand **guides** (2 posts/month).
